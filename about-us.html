About Us

Welcome to Outpace Steel Detailing

Outpace steel detailing has a vision to give quality detailing services for the benefit of steel fabricators, architects and other steel workers in the industry. Outpace Steel Detailing has a clear focus on providing services , using proven technology software's like SDS/2 and AutoCAD..

Outpace steel detailing provides high quality Structural Steel Detailing and Miscellaneous Steel Drafting Services for steel fabricators. Our experienced resources generate structural shop drawings using 3D Steel detailing software packages. We have a talented and excellent resource pool of detailers, checkers and project managers. Each assignment is handled by teams headed by a project manager with modelers, detailers and checkers with utmost care in translating the customer requirements into detailed drawings / fabrication drawings.

Who We are

Structural Steel Detailing

Structural steel detailing is a critical process of structural engineering and demands near-99% accuracy. Even a tiny error can lead to a loss of valuable time and money. Structural steel detailing is an important and mandatory process in all types of manufacturing and construction activities, such as erection of commercial and industrial buildings, The process of steel detailing is a vital 'communications link' that connects key professionals such as engineers, architects, contractors, fabricators and others—all of whom are individually and collectively responsible for the highest levels of accuracy at each stage.

Steel detailing involves the creation of detailed drawings for fabricators and Erector’s, The process involves two main types of drawings—shop drawings and erection drawings.
